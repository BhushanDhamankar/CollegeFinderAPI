COEP

Established in 1854, College of Engineering, Pune (COEP) is a public/ government college that is autonomous in nature. It is one of the oldest colleges in Asia that is aimed at offering quality education in the fields of Engineering and Technology. COEP is recognised by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA).

JSPM'S RSCOE

Rajarshi Shahu College of Engineering is one among the hallmark institutes of pune, established in 2001 with the aim of providing quality technical education and excellence in the ever expanding horizon of technical revolution of 21st century. The institute is becoming an icon in the field of engineering education in the state of Maharashtra. The Institute is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

PICT

Pune Institute of Computer Technology is a popular private owned institute which is known for its affordable fee structure. The institute was established in 1983 and is affiliated to SPPU, Pune. The institute offers B.E and M.E courses in various fields mainly focusing in the computer science field. The campus of the university is located in Pune, Maharashtra.

MIT Alandi

The MIT Academy of Engineering (MITAOE) was established under the prestigious MIT group of schools in Pune. The college has recently become the third institute in Pune which has been awarded with Academic autonomy by UGC. On top of that, the institute has been accredited with A grade by NAAC and also by NBA for all its programs. The institute offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in the field of engineering and research.

D.Y. Patil

D. Y. Patil College of Engineering was established in the year 1984 and is affiliated to SavitriBai Phule Pune University. The College is recognized by DTE and approved by AICTE. It is also accredited Grade A by NAAC. The College offers UG and PG courses in numerous specializations. The admission in these courses is based on Entrance Exams. The college also provides placement opportunities to students.
